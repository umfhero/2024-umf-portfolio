I'm a
Cyber Security and Digital Forensics Student

My passion lies in penetration testing, investigating cyber incidents, and understanding the intricacies of digital forensics. I have a strong foundation in ethical hacking, network security, and digital investigations, which I continue to build upon through both academic and personal projects.

I also undertook various modules during my studies, such as OOP (Java) and mathematics including topics like Functions, Linear Algebra, Quadratics, Statistics, Probability, Poisson & Normal Distributions, Proofs, Set Theory, Logs & Matrices.
