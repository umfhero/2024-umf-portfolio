  • PS5 Web Automation

    While still attending high school, I miraculously, unknowingly formed a strong connection towards computing through a client I found on social media. After replying to a post they made about a python job they needed help with, I managed to lay an agreement on a script to fully automate this process of buying a product for them to later make a profit on, after agreeing on a price, I eventually managed to create a script in a week while still attending school to make back what I made in a month from Cgtrader.

    In more detail, I managed to utilize my previous foundation in Python to begin creating what it seems now, quite a sophisticated automation bot. To assist me in this, I began getting familiar with tools such as Selenium that helped me control the web aspect of the automation which alongside targeting the XPath of classes, I could control actions and directions. This current stage of web automation is very cheap and basic and very common in this community, but at the time, I wanted to push myself due to the pure enjoyment of what I was doing, in doing so, I managed to work on allowing the bot to run every hour of the day, every day.

    To do this, I began working on bot detection bypassing, which included a handful of methods used such as the introduction of random paused which worked quite a bit on most websites but to push this more I began learning about IP address reputation. This was a major road bump, but to easily not get flagged on a website's system, changing the bots IP through a rotation system was as simple as connecting a Virtual Private Network into my system using this alongside the pausing feature. At the end of this, I managed to create a legal, undetectable automation bot that could perform multiple purchases of the Playstaion-5 console when the stock had been refilled.
